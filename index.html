Gym Therapy Experience is a Bootstrap‑built concept site that blends fitness, recovery, and real physical therapy into one accessible, energetic space. Every session is followed by access to in‑house recovery stations—TENS units, saunas, hot/cold compress therapy, plunges, and professional‑level PT massages—creating an environment where getting fit and getting well finally meet without the high cost of traditional therapy.
